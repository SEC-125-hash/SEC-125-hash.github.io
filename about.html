About SEC – Empowering Students to Succeed

Who We Are

SEC (Self Employment Company) is a student-first financial solution founded in Tanzania in 2023 by Jerome and Boniface. Our mission is to help students stay in school by giving them fast access to small loans, and to teach them financial independence by offering investment opportunities through shareholding. Whether you need emergency money or want to grow your savings — SEC is here for you.

Our Vision

To become the leading student financial empowerment platform in East Africa.

Our Mission

To support students financially through smart loans and investments, so they can succeed in their education without dropping out.

Our Core Values

Trust – We keep our promises.
Speed – Approvals in 24 hours.
Growth – We invest in student futures.
Support – Our agents are students too.

How We're Growing

Since launching in 2024, SEC has helped over 200 students across 12 campuses in Tanzania. We’re expanding through trusted campus agents and giving more students the power to control their financial future.
